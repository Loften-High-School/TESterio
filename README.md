What is our Game about?
The game that our group is making is a small platformer with a main focus of movement and travelling across an area. Our main focus is the movement and randomization in the game, with randomization it promotes high replayability with people not knowing what’s next. Our game is also inspired by Celeste Classic and Pico 8. We mainly made this with the idea of Speed running because children these days have watched many videos on media platforms and the most popular ones of younger kids are manhunts and speed runs, with our game being a speedrunning game this will most likely be the best idea.
